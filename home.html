The Skatepark That Makes Learning Fun!

Are you ready to take your skateboarding skills to the next level? Look no further than the New Buffalo Physics Skatepark! This one-of-a-kind skatepark combines the science of physics with the art of skateboarding, giving you the perfect way to understand and refine your skills.

At the New Buffalo Physics Skatepark, you can use a unique QR code system to learn about the physics of skateboarding and how it applies to everything else. By scanning the codes, you can access interactive tutorials that will help you understand the science behind your skateboarding moves.

Come to the New Buffalo Physics Skatepark today for a whole new experience in skateboarding!

What To Do In The Skatepark

The skatepark currently has five QR codes on or near different skatepark elements. Simply scan a QR code to learn all about the physics involved in skateboarding on that element.

Each QR-coded page will have a challenge at the end for you to practice and perfect with your skateboard. The challenge will involve understanding and utilizing the physics you have learned.
